ABOUT ME

Cheng Li is my official name. However my friends would love to call me Luphia (I made this name!). I was born in China and my first English name my teacher gave to me is Sophia which means wisdom and skill in Greek. Some years later, I was addicted to a song named Lucky, sang by Britney Spears. Then I decided to change my English name to Lucky (Sounds ridiculous, right? Because at that time in China, English name is just a code. It doesn't mean anything.). Well, after all Lucky is not a real name. Therefore, I choose a combination of Sophia and Lucky. Ta-da! Here comes Luphia.


Been working as a UI Developer for about 2 years, I finally find my interest in data analysis. I'm willing to devote my time and energy on learning and upgrading new coding languages and advanced skills. No matter how difficult or dull it is, in my eyes, they all become interesting because I ❤ it.


Meanwhile I can see the bright potential future in data field. In nowadays, data is no longer only number, but information as well. The resposibility of a data analyst is to turn data into informatin, information into sight, sight into business decision.


People encounter problems everyday and we keep fix them. I cannot be expected to know everything. But What I can be expected to know is how I would quickly get an answer or fill a gap in my knowledge.
